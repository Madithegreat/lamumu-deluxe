How to Play

  • Survive for 10 minutes — if the chain reaches the skull, you lose.
  • Aim with mouse/touch. Click / tap to shoot.
  • Swap the next ball with Q (desktop) or double-tap (mobile).
  • Match 3+ of the same color to clear.
  • Powerups (pop to trigger): Reverse (5s), Slow (7s), Bomb (area clear).
